Story About Us

White Tigers Club aims to provide beginners with a sound grounding in the basics of Fencing, Karate, Judo, Real Aikido, Yoga among other martial arts.

We are a friendly club base in Kasarani stadium.If you are interested in coming along to try the sport for the first time, kindly dont hesitate to contact us . We also aim to develop the skills of more experienced martial artists with a view to build a culture of success and fun.

We also offer home school programs and afterschool programs. Private Lessons are available in blocked times scheduled throughout the day when the club is open.

Fencing

We have classes for every fencing level along with special classes to work on footwork/Drills.

Workout

For all your full body workout needs without need for accessories

Yoga

Yoga for teams, kids ,adult, private and corporate all under one club.

Weight Loss
