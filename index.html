Story About Us

Kasarani Club aims to provide beginners with a sound grounding in the basics of Tong Ill Modo, Yoga among other martial arts.We aim to focus on your well being .

We are a friendly club base in Kasarani stadium.If you are interested in coming along to try the sport for the first time, kindly don't hesitate to contact us . We also aim to develop the skills of more experienced martial artists with a view to build a culture of success and fun.

We also offer home school programs and afterschool programs. Private Lessons are available in blocked times scheduled throughout the day when the club is open.

Yoga and Meditation

We welcome you to our power yoga sessions that are full of surprises from guest teachers to awesome poses. Come experience true connection, from within .

Mixed Martial Arts

We bring in a special kind of martial arts(Tong Ill Modo) for teams, kids ,adult, private and corporate all under one club.

Workout

For all your full body workout needs without need for accessories.We offer martial arts classes that will help you be able to defend yourself and those you care about . Use your opponent to your advantage

Weight Loss

Get more active with our out door activities eg hikes,mountain climbing , bike ridding, etc
